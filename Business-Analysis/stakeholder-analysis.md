# Stakeholder Analysis

## Primary Stakeholders

### Lara Pharmacy Network
- **Role**: Primary beneficiary and implementation partner
- **Interest**: Reduce medication spoilage, improve delivery efficiency
- **Impact**: 45 pharmacies across Buryatia

### Buryatia Ministry of Health
- **Role**: Regulatory oversight and public health coordination
- **Interest**: Improve healthcare access in remote regions
- **Impact**: 20,000+ rural residents

### Remote Village Communities
- **Role**: End beneficiaries
- **Interest**: Reliable access to life-saving medications
- **Impact**: Healthcare outcomes improvement

## Secondary Stakeholders

### Technology Providers
- IoT sensor manufacturers
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Mobile network operators

### Government Agencies
- Customs and regulatory bodies
- Transportation authorities
- Emergency services
